Documentation for User Data Deletion and Account Removal

Deleting Your Account

To completely delete your account and all associated data from our application:

  1. Go to Your Profile: Access your profile by logging into the application.
  2. Access Settings: In your profile, locate and click on the 'Settings' option.
  3. Delete Account: Within Settings, find and select the 'Delete Account' option.
  4. Confirm Deletion: You will be prompted to confirm your choice. Click 'Yes' to proceed. This action will permanently delete your account and all related information from our application.

Requesting Data Deletion Without Account Removal

If you wish to delete some or all of your data without deleting your account:

  1. Edit Your Profile: Go to your profile and click on 'Edit Profile'.
  2. Update Information: Modify or remove the information in your profile. The information in your profile is the only copy we have, so any changes you make will be immediately reflected in the data we store.
